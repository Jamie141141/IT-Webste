My computer is running slow. What can I do?

There are a few steps you can try to speed up your computer:

  1. Close any unnecessary programs or tabs that may be running in the background.
  2. Run a malware scan to make sure there are no malicious programs slowing down your computer.
  3. Defragment your hard drive to help optimize its performance.
  4. Consider upgrading your computer's hardware, such as adding more RAM or a faster processor.

How do I troubleshoot internet connectivity issues?

If you are experiencing internet connectivity issues, there are a few steps you can try:

  1. Restart your router and modem.
  2. Check for any outages in your area by contacting your internet service provider.
  3. Make sure your computer's wireless adapter is turned on and working properly.
  4. Try connecting to the internet using an ethernet cable to rule out any wireless issues.
